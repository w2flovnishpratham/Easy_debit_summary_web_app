HOW IT WORKS

4 Steps to Extract Transactions

Process any Indian bank statement in under a minute.

  1. 01
    Select Your Bank

    Choose the issuing bank — HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Yes Bank, or IndusInd.

  2. 02
    Upload Statement PDF

    Attach the bank-provided PDF. You can add multiple statements from different banks at once.

  3. 03
    Enter PDF Password if locked

    Most banks protect PDFs with DOB or Customer ID. We'll show a hint per bank.

  4. 04
    Extract & Download

    Click Upload and Extract to process. Download clean CSV or open the full summary dashboard.
